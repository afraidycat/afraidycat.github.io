Step-by-Step

Haha, this is a funny purchase. My wife wanted to join a Disney DVD club to collect movies for our nephew and nieces. Below are the steps I took to order these DVD’s:

1) Reviewed the Disney Movie Club pamphlet – specifically, I wanted to find out what was required from me when joining. I was also trying to find out how I would order online instead of completing a mail-in form.

2) Found the URL and member code to join the club online instead of mailing

3) Visited the site Disney DVD Club site and entered my special code

4) Selected the movies I wanted to purchase – This was difficult, since my wife wanted specific movies and they were not all available. I chose the movies that were classics so the kids could enjoy timeless movies.

5) Entered my account information (including Name, Address, Credit Card)

6) Reviewed the terms and conditions of my purchase – I reviewed this carefully to ensure that what I thought I was getting when reviewing the pamphlet, was exactly what I was getting.

7) Completed my order

8) Looked for my email confirming the order was complete
